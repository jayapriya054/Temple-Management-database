Problem statement
----Most of the ancient temples in Tamil Nadu are in decline due to environmental damage, lack of attention, and insufficient documentation.
----The current system for managing temples lacks a detailed and centralized way to track temple conditions and restoration activities.

Solution

----In the proposed database, Real-time information on temple conditions, such as environmental hazards and structural health, will be tracked and centralized.
----It will assist in monitoring and managing restoration projects, ensuring that work is planned, completed on schedule, and within budget.
----Also, The system will allow stakeholders to submit reports, images, and insights in order to improve cooperation and decision-making for temple conservation.
